Founder's Note

When I started The Painted Muse, it came from a very personal place — a deep love for art and a belief that creativity deserves to be seen, celebrated, and shared with the world.

Coming from a background in engineering, I always found myself drawn toward colors, stories, emotions, and the way art can connect people beyond language. Over time, I realized that many incredibly talented artists struggle not because they lack skill, but because they lack visibility, opportunities, and a supportive creative community.

That thought became the foundation of The Painted Muse.

This platform was created to bring artists, creators, and art lovers together in one inspiring space — a space where creativity feels limitless, collaborations feel natural, and every artist feels valued. Whether it's through workshops, retreats, exhibitions, or creative conversations, our goal is to build experiences that inspire people to create fearlessly and connect authentically.

At The Painted Muse, we believe art is not just something you make — it's something you feel. It has the power to heal, to express, to transform, and to bring people closer to themselves and each other.

Every event, every project, and every idea we create is rooted in passion, community, and creativity.

Thank you for being here and for becoming a part of this journey. Your support helps us continue building a world where artists are encouraged to dream bigger, create freely, and feel truly seen.

With love,

Nidhika Wankhede

Founder & CEO, The Painted Muse
