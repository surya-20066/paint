Madhubani Tradition

Meera Devi

Meera Devi is a 5th generation Madhubani artist from Mithila, Bihar. Her intricate brushwork captures the essence of ancestral forest dreamscapes. With over 40 years of experience, her works have been displayed in galleries worldwide, bridging the gap between traditional storytelling and modern appreciation.
